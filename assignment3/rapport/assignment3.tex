\section{Question 1.1}

We wish to show that
\[
    \frac{d}{da} \frac{a}{1+|a|} = \frac{1}{(1+|a|)^2}.
\]

We have that

\begin{align}
    \frac{d}{da} \frac{a}{1+|a|} &= \frac{1 \cdot (1+|a|) - a \cdot \sgn(a)}{(1+|a|)^2} \\
                                 &= \frac{(1 + |a|) - |a|}{(1+|a|)^2} \\
                                 &= \frac{1}{(1+|a|)^2},
\end{align}

as desired.
